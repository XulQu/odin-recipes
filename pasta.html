Pasta Recipe

This wonderful pasta will make anyone's mouth water

This recipe follows a great trendy meal option with a twist of lemon and garlic

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes 300g
  • Spaghetti
  • Oregano
  • Vegetable Stock 500ml
  • Muscles / Clams 500g
  • Lemons x3
  • 1 Cup Wine
  • Basil
  • 1 Tsp Oil
  • 2 Onions White
  • Black Pepper & Salt

Steps to Prepare

  1. Par-cook the muscles in garlic, butter and lemon juice for 10mins until they begin to open, place to the side
  2. Chop onions & tomatoes, place into pan with 1tsp of oil on low heat
  3. Add to the veggies your tomatoes, puree and veggie stock
  4. Add in 1tsp salt, 1tsp pepper & a generous pinch of Oregano, add wine and cook down
  5. Add the spaghetti to a pot and allow to boil while the sauce nears completion
  6. Add in muscles which were set to the side, you can add in clam meat to add more depth to the meal
  7. Allow the sauce to complete when oils and fats rise to the top, add in a 1/4 cup of pasta water as well as the spaghetti which should be aldente.
  8. Serve while fresh and hot, add Basil to the pasta to add a nice finish to the meal
