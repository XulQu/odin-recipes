Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious Lasagna Recipe for cozy dinners

This recipe follows a classical Italian layout, with a modern substitute for convience. It's perfect for anyone looking to wow
their families with amazing food.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes 300g
  • Pasta Sheets 500g
  • Oregano
  • Vegetable Stock 500ml
  • Mince Meat 500g
  • 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Basil
  • 1 Tsp Oil
  • 2 Onions White
  • Cheese 500g

Steps to Prepare

  1. Chop onions & tomatoes, place into pan with 1tsp of oil on low heat

  2. Once veggies are cooked down, add in mince and let it brown and simmer before adding stock

  3. Add in 1tsp salt, 1tsp pepper & a generous pinch of Oregano

  4. While mince bubbles, create the Rou, by adding flour slowly to milk or water and whisk it, once thick place to the side

  5. Layer pasta sheets into over ready dish, and layer mince, followed by rou, one final layer place cheese grated on top with rou.

  6. Let it cook in the oven at 200 degrees C. for 40 to 60 mins until cooked through and golden brown
