Burger Recipe

This is the best burger recipe for the game, a good lunch or parties with your friends

This recipe follows all the classical traits for a good burger & a great meal

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince 1KG
  • x6 Burger Buns White
  • Oregano
  • Breadcrumps 200g
  • Eggs x2
  • Garlic Crushed x4 Tbsp
  • Bacon Bits 200g
  • Cheese Slices
  • Baby Letuce
  • 2 tsp Oil
  • 2 Onions Red
  • Tomatoes x3

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix Beef Mince, Oregano, Garlic, Bacon Bits, and Breadcrumps in a bowl, bind with eggs

  2. Take Beef mix and turn into patties by rolling on a board into circles and falttening in palm

  3. Heat grill on high heat until noticable, add in oil and seer patties

  4. Cook patties until firm and cooked through

  5. Toast Buns, add layers: Letuce, patty, cheese, tomato sliced, Letuce sliced and finally close the Burger

  6. Enjoy with your favourite sauce, jam or anything which will compliment the meat
